About Fipra

Finsbury International Policy & Regulatory Advisers is Europe's leading and most extensive public affairs consultancy network advising clients on a range of government relations and regulatory policy issues. Over the last 15 years, Fipra has expanded across the globe and now operates in over 60 countries both in Europe and Internationally.

In Europe, we are the only consultancy with top level public affairs advisers not only in Brussels, but throughout the Member States of the European Union. In addition, we have experienced public affairs advisers in a number of European Economic Area Countries and EU candidate countries, as well as many of the EU's neighbouring countries.

By clicking on individual regions and countries to the right of this screen, you will find more details about Fipra in each of the various locations and countries in which we operate. You can also find useful information and links to the principal government, media and other relevant sites in the country you choose.

We are known both for the seniority and expertise of our staff and for getting results. Many of our staff and Special Advisers have personal experience of having served national governments, parliaments and regulatory bodies, or have held senior positions in industry, trade associations, or consumer organisations. All of them are experts in the political and regulatory process, not least because they have experienced them from many different perspectives at first hand.

Further information on the public affairs services that we provide can be found here. In addition, Fipra's Code of Conduct, derived from many years of public policy experience, governs our thinking and helps explain what we do and how it is done.

The Fipra network is run and co-ordinated by Fipra International Ltd, which is also responsible for licensing the Fipra brand and for the group's marketing and sales.
